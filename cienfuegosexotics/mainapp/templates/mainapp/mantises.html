Mantises

One of nature's most amazing creatures. This is a bug with a personality.

This creature will change your perception of insects. They love to be held and will gladly walk in your hands and on your head. They turn their heads to look at you and follow you with their gaze.

It's absolutely terrifying to watch them snatch flies out of the air and devour them whole. It's like watching an African Lions eat a buffallo on National Geographic. These bugs are peaceful with humans but merciless when it comes to other bugs.

A fantastic and interesting pet. They live through the summer and pass away in the fall. Stunningly beautiful, interesting, short-lived creatures.
