Peppers In Stock

We start our peppers from seed in our greenhouse in December. It's hard work. The baby plants require lots of heat, light and attention and we have to constantly fight off bug pests and plant diseases. Some plants die along the way.

When you buy a plant from us, you don't need to waste time with this boring hard work of turning a seeling into a mature plant.We'll give you a big healthy plant that's almost sure to give you a huge harvest. If you take care of your plant right, it can live 3 or more years!

Habanada
It's Not Spicy!

A very hard to find pepper plant. Looks like a habanero pepper, you'll probably be scared to bite into one of these. But it's actually very sweet! A very interesting plant and wonderful conversation piece for your back yard.

Sweet Carolina Bell
Delicious!

A delicious bell pepper. Large, sweet, firm peppers. Typically cost about $2 per pound at the grocery store, but you'll have unlimited peppers right there in your yard or on your fire escape!

Dragon Cayenne
This one's no joke!

A properly spicy pepper. Long thin finger peppers, perfect for your favorite recipes or sauces.

Carolina Reaper
The Big Dog!

This is the hottest pepper in the world. One pepper will be enough to last you all winter. Really a dangerous plant, don't keep this around children or pets. Proceed with caution and have fun.
